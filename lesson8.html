Lesson Overview

In the last lesson, you learned how to make your website responsive. Now, it's time to put everything together and build your own multi-page website as your final project!

Project Requirements

  • At least three pages (e.g., Home, About, Contact)
  • Navigation menu linking all pages
  • Use of forms, tables, images, and media
  • Styling with CSS, including layout, colors, and typography
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • At least one animation or transition

Tips

  • Plan your site structure before you start coding.
  • Test your site on different devices and screen sizes.
  • Review previous lessons if you get stuck.

Congratulations!
